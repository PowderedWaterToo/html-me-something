Little Boy: Learning How To Live With Humans

Rogi came home with us in November 2016. For reasons I won't get into--but that you can read more about--his name had to start with a J. So instead of Rogi, which is the name we wanted (it is Igor backwards; Igor was my childhood dog), we gave him the "official" name of Javierogi vom Mittelwest (Mittelwest is the kennel name).
Raising a puppy (and living with humans) was a learning experience for everyone.
